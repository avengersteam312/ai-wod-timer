AI WOD Timer turns any workout description into a ready-to-run timer in seconds. Just type or paste your WOD — the AI detects the format and sets up every interval automatically.

No more manually counting rounds, setting intervals, or doing math mid-warmup. Whether you're programming for yourself or coaching a class, paste the workout and go.

Supported workout formats:
• AMRAP — as many rounds as possible with automatic countdown
• EMOM — every minute on the minute with per-minute interval tracking
• Tabata — 20/10 work-rest cycles with configurable rounds
• For Time — countdown timer with round tracking
• Intervals — custom work/rest periods with rep targets
• Work/Rest — simple alternating work and rest rounds
• Stopwatch — open-ended count-up for max-effort sets
• Custom — any format that doesn't fit a standard template

Whiteboard to timer in one tap:
Point your camera at the whiteboard or pick a photo from your gallery — the AI reads the workout and sets up the timer automatically. No typing required.

Record your workouts:
Film your sets with the built-in video recorder. A live timer overlay shows your round count, work/rest phase, and remaining time while you record. Save the video to your gallery or share directly to Instagram, TikTok, or anywhere else.

Built for athletes:
• Audio cues at every interval transition — no need to watch the screen
• Works offline — timers saved locally, synced to the cloud when back online
• Workout history with timestamps
• Manual timer mode — no account needed for basic use
• Cloud sync across devices when signed in
