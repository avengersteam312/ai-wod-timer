## How to Use These

1. Copy any workout above
2. Paste into the AI Workout Timer
3. Click "Generate Timer"
4. Watch the AI parse it and create a smart timer!

The AI should correctly identify:
- Workout type (AMRAP, EMOM, For Time, etc.)
- Duration or time cap
- Movements with reps and weights
- Round structure
- Rest periods

Try modifying these workouts or creating your own variations to test the AI's parsing capabilities!
