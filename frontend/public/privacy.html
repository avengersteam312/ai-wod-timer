Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2025

AI WOD Timer ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials through our authentication provider (Supabase).

Workout Data: We collect workout descriptions you enter into the app for parsing and timer configuration.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To process your workout descriptions using AI (OpenAI) to generate timer configurations
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data
  • To improve our services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: To process and parse workout descriptions. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: For hosting the application. Vercel Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. Workout data submitted for parsing is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
